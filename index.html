Tristan

Tristan Cooke

A true pioneer, with an unflinching love for Psytrance, Tristan is constantly placing himself at the very cutting edge, always pushing the envelope and creating a massive wake of psychedelic inspiration around him as he forges new sounds and techniques. His infinite energy and enthusiasm for psychedelic music, the way of life that intertwines with it, and his larger than life stage presence, has rightfully earned Tristan legendary status amongst a very dedicated and adoring global fan base.
